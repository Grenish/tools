Welcome to **Tools** – a fresh take on component libraries designed for developers who value freedom, simplicity, and real-world applicability.

In a landscape dominated by restrictive or overly complex libraries, Tools stands out by prioritizing accessibility and adaptability. Unlike options locked behind paywalls or burdened by rigid, closed-source designs that sacrifice usability for scale, Tools empowers you with components that are intuitive and easy to customize.

Built on the solid foundation of shadcn/ui principles, our curated collection of handcrafted components and utilities is crafted to be:

- **Free and open**  
  Fully accessible without barriers – no paywalls, hidden features, or gated content.

- **Human-first**  
  Designed for practical, everyday use, not just polished demos or showcases.

- **Simple and composable**  
  Straightforward to grasp, tweak, and weave into your existing projects.

- **Production-ready**  
  Reliable, stable elements that form the backbone of your applications.

Tools doesn't dictate a one-size-fits-all system. Instead, it offers modular, flexible pieces that fit seamlessly into your workflow. Pick what you need, customize it to suit your vision, and build without constraints.

Our mission is clear: to provide high-quality, frictionless components that enhance your development experience and bring your ideas to life effortlessly.
