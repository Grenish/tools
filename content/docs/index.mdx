Welcome to **Tools** — a modern component library built for developers who prioritize flexibility, clarity, and real-world usability.

In an ecosystem often shaped by restrictive licensing or unnecessary complexity, Tools takes a different approach. It focuses on delivering accessible, adaptable components without imposing rigid structures or limiting customization. The goal is to provide building blocks that integrate naturally into your workflow rather than dictate it.

Inspired by the principles of shadcn/ui, this collection emphasizes:

- **Open and accessible**
  Completely free to use, with no paywalls, hidden features, or gated functionality.

- **Developer-centric design**
  Built for real-world scenarios, prioritizing usability over superficial presentation.

- **Composable and intuitive**
  Easy to understand, extend, and integrate into existing systems.

- **Production-ready**
  Stable, reliable components suitable for real applications at scale.

Tools is not a prescriptive framework. It is a flexible toolkit that allows you to select only what you need and adapt it to your specific requirements.

This project is continuously evolving. Ongoing effort is being put into refining existing components and introducing new ones to ensure quality, consistency, and practical value. The aim is to deliver a dependable resource that developers can rely on while steadily improving it over time.
